Anatomy of a Ransomware Attack

Ransomware is a chain of silent, calculated events.

01
Infiltration

Infiltration (The Trojan Horse)

It always starts with something harmless. A finance employee receives an email... The email contains an attachment named Invoice_March.pdf.exe.

Key Takeaway: The human factor is the first line of defense.

02
Exploration

Exploration (The Silent Enemy)

The malware spends days or even weeks silently walking through the network to map the infrastructure.

Key Takeaway: Attackers prefer stealth over speed.
